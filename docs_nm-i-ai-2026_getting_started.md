# Getting Started

Welcome to the `NM i AI 2026` documentation. The competition runs from **March 19 at 18:00 CET** to **March 22 at 15:00 CET** — `69` hours of non-stop AI challenges with a `1,000,000 NOK` prize pool.

## The Three Tasks

`NM i AI 2026` consists of three independent AI challenges. Your overall score is the average of your normalized scores across all three tasks (`33%` each).

| Task | Type | Submission |
|---|---|---|
| `Tripletex` | AI accounting agent | HTTPS API endpoint |
| `Astar Island` | Norse world prediction | REST API predictions |
| `NorgesGruppen Data` | Grocery shelf object detection | Code upload (`ZIP`) |

## Prize pool

### Placement Prizes
- 1st Place - 400,000 NOK
- 2nd Place - 300,000 NOK
- 3rd Place - 200,000 NOK
- Best U23 TeamU23 - 100,000 NOK

The U23 prize is awarded to the highest-ranking team where all members are under 23 years of age at the competition end date (March 22, 2026). The U23 prize is combinable with placement prizes — a U23 team that places in the top 3 receives both prizes.

### How It Works
- Your overall score is the average of normalized scores across all tasks (equal weight).
- Prizes are split equally among team members and paid out individually.
- Prizes are paid out gross (no tax withheld by the organizer).
- All team members must be verified via Vipps to be eligible for prizes.

## Quick Start

- Sign in at `app.ainm.no` with Google
- Create or join a team (max `4` members)
- Complete `Vipps` verification for prize eligibility
- Explore the task documentation in the sidebar
- Submit to as many tasks as you can
